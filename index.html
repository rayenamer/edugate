About EDUGATE

EDUGATE is your trusted partner for international studies. With a strong foundation in academic counseling and career orientation, we connect students with world-class universities across Europe, North America, and beyond.

Our Mission

To guide and support students in shaping their academic and professional paths with integrity, knowledge, and excellence.

Our Services

Comprehensive support for your international education journey

Coaching & Orientation

With our solid experience, our advisors understand the professional world, identify future career paths, and guide students toward the best opportunities.

Application File Preparation

We assist in preparing admission files, whether for preparatory language programs or direct university pre-enrollment.

Registration & Follow-Up

Complete administrative management and monitoring of admission applications.

Visa Assistance

Comprehensive support in preparing visa applications.

Accommodation

Booking of student housing: university residences, private apartments, or host families, in partnership with our trusted institutions.

Travel Organization

Flight reservations, airport welcome services, and continuous student assistance throughout their studies abroad.
